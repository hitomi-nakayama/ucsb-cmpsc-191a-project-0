Update: 12/5/99 Y2K Bug Reminder

The new millennium is approaching quickly. Don't forget to take precautionary measures to protect yourself and your electronic devices.

  • Turn off all computers before December 31st.
  • Have a week's worth of cash on hand.
  • Have a few day's worth of food and water in your pantry.
  • Fill your car full of gas.
  • Buy batteries and a battery powered radio.

Though some reports may be a little overblown, it's better to be safe than sorry. Keep up to date on the latest Y2K news on the New York Times Year 2000 Problem Index.
